About Event

Introducing IGE Community Series '23 Valorant, an exciting new Esports series launched by InGame Esports (IGE). The tournament series will feature separate competitions in Bangladesh, Nepal, Pakistan and Sri Lanka.

 Organised by IGE for the first time, this series aims to promote Esports at the community level in each country and serves as a great opportunity for gamers both new and old to gain exposure and recognition.  

IGE is proud to introduce a new roadmap for the future of Esports in South Asia and allow gamers in the region to showcase their skills and talent.
