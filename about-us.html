PlayUnite

(Fanverse Technologies)

As games become the dominant way people spend time, we believe some of the most innovative products will be built around player communities PlayUnite is building a gamified, play to earn esports community powered by web3. An exclusive limited member community, where players have fun social identities & fair economic incentives for the time & skills they devote to the game. Just like having a cool in game alter ego, players part of Rhym community have a digital incarnation or avatars of their own gaming skills, passion, progression in esports Avatars evolve with player’s performance in tournaments - giving them access to more liked minded community, matching them with skilled players & giving them higher earnings in tournaments. Players own & grow this avatar as a personal asset, increasing it’s intrinsic social & economic value with their gameplay. So now a gamer can show their skills, achievements & monetise from it by selling or lending their avatars to other players who want to be part of the special community & play in its tournaments With “Play to Earn” PlayUnite also has more equitable & value based rewards in tournaments. Economy will be powered by Rhym’s native crypto token/currency which players can own & hold to earn or spend it for the assets & experiences in the community. We are excited about creating this rich immersive experience for the esports community & at the same time help them grow to be a strong professional esports player
